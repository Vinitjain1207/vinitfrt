About Us.

My name is Vinit .I set upon my journey to design my dream home. It didn’t take me long to realise how fragmented the industry was and how factors like identifying the right professionals, coordination of tasks, and ensuring fair price and quality were pain points faced by many.

Determined to fix this and offer a happier experience to homeowners, I built a much needed bridge for the industry. Lavish was founded in 2024. It is now the trusted brand for complete home interior design and renovation for thousands of homeowners.
